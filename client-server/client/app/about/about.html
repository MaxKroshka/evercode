Nevercode is driven by the question, "How do you become a world class programmer?" We aim to help take great programmers and make them wizards by allowing them to easily store, edit, and retrieve code throughout their careers. Instead of rifiling through old project files for your interesting or useful algorithms, test arrays, and boilerplates try pulling them straight into your text editor with <N>ever:code
